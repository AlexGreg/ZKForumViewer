ZK SpreadSheet : Hide a range of rows is bugging

ff06829s
8 Jun 2012 07:21:31 GMT
8 Jun 2012 07:21:31 GMT

Hello,

I'am coding with ZK SpreadSheet and I have display bugs.
When I go to the end of the page on the browser (scroll completely to down), then I click on a "minus" icon (collapse row group) to hide the last 2 rows at the bottom of the spreadsheet (which are at the bottom of the screen too), and then I click on the "plus" (uncollapse row group), the displaying of the uncollapsed rows is buggy.
Last group of rows are half displayed, why ?
Where as at first time (all uncollapsed), the last rows are well displayed !

Can you help me ... thx.

ff06829s
12 Jun 2012 15:50:50 GMT
12 Jun 2012 15:50:50 GMT

It's OK.
The bug was coming from the attributs vflex="1" and hflex="1" set on the <groupbox> component.
I removed them and the display bug dissapeared.

Thx.
